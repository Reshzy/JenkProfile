About Me

Hello! My name is Jane, but I'm known online as jenky. I'm a passionate artist from the Philippines dedicated to creating compelling visual content.

I work with content creators and serve as one of the content creators for the official Mobile Legends: Bang Bang page. My journey began in Paulina Homes, Dalipuga, Iligan City, where my two older brothers introduced me to the digital world and inspired my creative pursuits.

My aspirations include becoming a recognized artist, securing a stable career in my field, and reconnecting with my childhood friends scattered across Mindanao and beyond.
